        Does renters insurance cover moving?
        If you continue to rent at your new location, you just need to update your existing renters policy with your new address. It is also a good time to review your coverage and make sure it still meets your needs. If you are buying a house and getting a USAA homeowners policy, you can cancel your renters insurance online once your homeowners coverage is active and your personal items are delivered to your home. Be sure to reactivate your renters policy if the closing on your home is delayed or you do not end up purchasing the house. If you are using a professional moving company and you have a shipping or storage contract, your belongings are covered if they are lost, damaged or destroyed during the move. Keep in mind, your stuff is not covered for breaking, marring or scratching. Consider purchasing coverage offered by the moving company for additional protection.
